Caesar Salad

Classic Caesar Salad Recipe

A timeless favorite, this Caesar salad combines crisp romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, and a rich, creamy dressing packed with garlic, Parmesan, and a hint of anchovy for that signature flavor. Topped with freshly grated Parmesan and optional grilled chicken or shrimp, this salad is a satisfying side or light main course. Perfect for dinner parties or an easy, refreshing meal.

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 large head romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

For the Dressing

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 clove garlic (minced or grated)
  • 2 anchovy fillets (optional, finely chopped or mashed)
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2-3 tbsp olive oil

Optional Add-Ins:

  • Grilled chicken, shrimp, or steak
  • Soft-boiled or poached egg

Classic Caesar Salad Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Dressing

  1. In a small mixing bowl, combine the mayonnaise, garlic, anchovy fillets (if using), Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, and Parmesan cheese.
  2. Whisk until smooth.
  3. Gradually whisk in the olive oil to achieve a creamy consistency
  4. Taste and season with salt and pepper. Set aside.

Step 2: Prepare the Lettuce

  1. Wash and thoroughly dry the romaine lettuce. (Excess water can dilute the dressing.)
  2. Place the lettuce in a large salad bowl.

Step 3: Assemble the Salad

  1. Add the croutons to the bowl with the lettuce.
  2. Drizzle the prepared dressing over the salad. Start with a small amount and toss well, adding more if needed to coat the lettuce evenly.
  3. Sprinkle the freshly grated Parmesan cheese on top.

Step 4: Optional Add-Ins

  1. If adding protein, slice grilled chicken, shrimp, or steak into bite-sized pieces and arrange them on top of the salad.
  2. For a classic touch, add a soft-boiled or poached egg to the center.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Serve immediately while the croutons are crispy and the lettuce is fresh.
  2. Pair with a crusty baguette or enjoy as a light main dish!

This Caesar salad is a crowd-pleaser and can be easily customized to suit any occasion!
