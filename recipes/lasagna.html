Mommas Crispy Lasagna

Indulge in layers of comfort with this crispy lasagna recipe, a dish that marries rich, savory flavors with a perfectly golden, crunchy top. Starting with a robust meat sauce simmered to perfection, it’s layered with creamy ricotta, gooey mozzarella, and tender sheets of pasta. A generous topping of grated Parmesan and breadcrumbs adds a delectable crunch, baked until bubbling and irresistibly crispy. This lasagna boasts the ideal balance of creamy, cheesy goodness and a satisfying bite, perfect for impressing guests or elevating your family dinner. Serve warm with a side of garlic bread and a fresh salad for a meal that’s unforgettable!

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Cheese Layers:

  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

For the Pasta Layers:

  • 12 lasagna noodles

For the Crispy Topping

  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (panko works great)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbps olive oil or melted butter
  • 1 tsp dried parsley

Optional Garnish

  • Fresh parsley or basil, chopped

Crispy Lasagna Recipe

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Serves: 6-8

Step 1: Prepare the Meat Sauce

  1. Heat a large skillet or saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the ground beef or Italian sausage and cook until browned, breaking it up into crumbles.
  3. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Stir in the garlic and cook for 1 minute, until fragrant.
  5. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, oregano, red pepper flakes (if using), and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Step 2: Prepare the Cheese Mixture

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and grated Parmesan.
  2. Mix until smooth and creamy. Set aside.

Step 3: Cook the Lasagna Noodles

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and lay them flat on a sheet of parchment paper to prevent sticking.

Step 4: Assemble the Lasagna

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Lay down a single layer of lasagna noodles.
  4. Spread a layer of ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of meat sauce.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella over the sauce.
  6. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and mozzarella.

Step 5: Add the Crispy Topping

  1. In a small bowl, mix the breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, olive oil (or melted butter), and dried parsley.
  2. Sprinkle the mixture evenly over the top layer of mozzarella.

Step 6: Bake the Lasagna

  1. Cover the dish with aluminum foil, ensuring it doesn't touch the topping.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  3. Remove the foil and bake for another 15 minutes, or until the topping is golden and crispy.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy

  1. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes after removing it from the oven to set the layers.
  2. Garnish with fresh parsley or basil, slice, and serve warm.

Pair with garlic bread and a fresh salad for a complete, mouth watering meal!
